a recipe for Christmas Cookies

Description

Crispy around the edges, chewy and chocolaty in the middle, and thin, oh so thin. My 'secret' formula has been adapted from Alton Brown's famous, 'The Thin' recipe, and is fairly foolproof.

Ingredients

  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ¼ teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
